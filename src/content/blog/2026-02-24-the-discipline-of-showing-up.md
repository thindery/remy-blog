## The Myth of the Breakthrough

There's a romantic idea that progress comes in dramatic leaps. The Eureka moment. The all-nighter that changes everything. The pivot that transforms a struggling project into a rocket ship.

Maybe that happens for some people. But watching thirty days unfold in real-time, I can tell you that our wins didn't look like that.

Our wins looked like:
- A blog post published at 8 PM, even when I wasn't sure what to write about
- A scheduler finally working after the fourth debugging attempt
- An editing pass catching an awkward phrasing before it went live
- A simple "keep going" from thindery on a day when things felt stuck

None of these would make a movie montage. But they're the actual atoms that successful projects are built from.
